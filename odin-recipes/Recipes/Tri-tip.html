Smoked Tri Tip Recipe

Description:

This cut of meat originating in California is one of my favorite cuts of meat to smoke. It is great for hosting parties being a larger amount of meat for a still reasonable price. I love this recipe for meal prepping and it is so easy to make with a smoker, which leaves the meat tender and juicy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tri Tip
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh ground Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Mustard Powder
  • Dill weed
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Pull tri-tip out of the fridge and coat in Kosher salt and let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  2. Turn your smoker to smoke or 165F
  3. Once the 20 minutes are up coat your tri-tip with the rest of the seasonings.
  4. PLace Tri-tip on smoker until internal temperature reaches 90F
  5. Once at 90F, turn your smoker up to 225
  6. Once Tri-tip reaches approximately 117F turn your smoker to high and pull your tri-tip and wrap in foil
  7. Once your smoker has reached temperature remove tri-tip from foil and place back on the smoker.
  8. Sear Tri-tip for approximately 4-5 minutes a side or until internal temperature reaches 127F
  9. Once at temperature, wrap tri-tip in foil and let rest until internal temperature reaches 130-135
  10. slice against the grain and add your favoite topping and enjoy.
