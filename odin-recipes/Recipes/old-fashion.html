Classic Old Fashion

Description:

This classic Whiskey drink is one of my all time favorites. A smooth drink when added with an orange slice/rind or cocktail cherries to give a nice fruity finish. Created in 1880 as an ode to how they made the drink in the days of ol' from a bartender named James E. Pepper. He eventually took this drink to the Waldorf-Astoria hotel in New York where it became famous and remains the same today.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite Bourbon Whiskey
  • Coctail Bitters (I prefer Black walnut Bitters)
  • Cane sugar or Brown Sugar
  • Cocktail Cherries
  • Orange (for the peel)
  • Large Ice Cube
  • Low Ball Glass

Steps

  1. PLace sugar cube or brown sugar in glass
  2. wetten sugar with three to four dashes of bitters
  3. Add in a splash of water
  4. Muddle sugar until dissolved
  5. Pour one to two Oz. of whiskey (choice of strength)
  6. Stir
  7. Optional: before adding ice cube smoke with wood of choice
  8. add Ice cube and enjoy.
